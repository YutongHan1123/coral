Public Engagement

Our work is contingent upon the support and engagement of our communities. For this reason, we built a communications strategy to inform, engage, and win support from stakeholders in the City of Miami Beach. The formative research phase of this strategy included a survey of and in-depth interviews with Miami Beach residents.

This research assessed, among other things, residents’ beliefs about the damage waves cause to coasts and coastal property, and their knowledge about and attitudes towards green and gray infrastructure, including in particular corals. We are currently in the process of developing outreach materials to be distributed, including an informational short film, handouts, and an event (to be hosted in the fall).
