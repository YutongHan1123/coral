Local Actions We Can Take

You can take the following actions to mitigate these threats:

  • Manage Watersheds
    •
    • Reduce nutrient inputs (fertilizers, sewage, runoff)
    • Reduce pollution (chemicals, sediment, wastewater)
  • Stop Overfishing
    •
    • Support the expansion of Florida’s no-take marine reserves
  • Implement Sustainable Coastal Development
    •
    • Strengthen environmental oversight on coastal construction
  • Reduce/ Eliminate Single-use Plastics
    •
  • Engage in Responsible Boating and Fishing
    •

    • Use electric/ hybrid vehicles
    • Use public transport
    • Bike or walk whenever possible
    • Support slow food initiatives
    • Reduce/ eliminate meat consumption
  • Support Policies that Reflect Environmental Priorities
